# Parasitic Eel

- Family: Eel
- Original guide page: https://web.archive.org/web/20031219032012id_/http://alienfishexchange.com:80/guide/parasitic.html

## Notes

Parasitic eels take great skill and dedication to breed. They will eat almost anything, but they go really well with linguini and capers.
