# Manx Catfish

- Family: Catfish
- Original guide page: https://web.archive.org/web/20031219032012id_/http://alienfishexchange.com:80/guide/manx.html

## Notes

With only small fins and no tail, the manx catfish requires a lot of energy to move. This accounts for its outrageous appetite.
