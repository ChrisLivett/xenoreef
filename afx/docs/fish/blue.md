# Blue Sprat

- Family: Sprat
- Original guide page: https://web.archive.org/web/20031219032012id_/http://alienfishexchange.com:80/guide/blue.html

## Notes

Blue sprat are the largest and most valuable of the three main sprat types.

## Source

- Buy from AliFEx: Yes
- Bred from: Red Sprat & Green Sprat
- Mutated from: White Sprat (1-1)

## Life Cycle

- Birth weight: 2g
- Target weight: 30g
- Daily growth: 4g
- Fertile: Day 1
- Mutation: Day 7: Grey Sprat (1-1)
- Breeding:
  - Blue Sprat => White Sprat
  - Red Sprat => Green Sprat
  - Green Sprat => Red Sprat

## Feeding

- Prey: None
- Hunger: 20% of body weight per day
- Eating style: n/a
