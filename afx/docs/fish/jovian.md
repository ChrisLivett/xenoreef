# Jovian Piranha

- Family: Piranha
- Original guide page: https://web.archive.org/web/20031219032012id_/http://alienfishexchange.com:80/guide/jovian.html

## Notes

Often incorrectly called the jovial piranha, this small predator breeds with a wide range of species. Allowed to breed unchecked, the resulting offspring is the deadly ionic piranha.
