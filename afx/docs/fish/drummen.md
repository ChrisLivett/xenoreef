- Family: Bass
- Original guide page: https://web.archive.org/web/20031219032012id_/http://alienfishexchange.com:80/guide/drummen.html

## Notes

These awe-inspiring fish have an ego that's out of control and needs frequent feeding. When they're in season, all other bass simply cower in the corner of the tank.
