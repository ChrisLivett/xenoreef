# Rainbow Sprat

- Family: Sprat
- Original guide page: https://web.archive.org/web/20031219032012id_/http://alienfishexchange.com:80/guide/rainbow.html

## Notes

Rainbow sprat should be protected carefully, as they produce a wide range of interesting mutations after just two days.

## Source

- Buy from AliFEx: No
- Bred from: None
- Mutated from:
  - Nietzsches Philosofish (school)
  - Invisible Piranha (1-1)
  - Ultra-violet Sprat (school)
  - Grey Sprat (1-1)

## Life Cycle

- Birth weight: 30g
- Target weight: 35g
- Daily growth: 2g
- Fertile: Day 1
- Mutation: Day 3: 40% chance: Ultra-violet Sprat (school), 20% chance: Lesser Europan Shark (school), 20% chance: Coruscating Angelfish (school), 20% chance: Siamese Catfish (school)
- Breeding: - Blazing Angelfish => Iridescent Angelfish

## Feeding

- Prey: White Sprat
- Hunger: 30% of body weight per day
- Eating style: Smallest suitable prey
